About Shri G.S Institute of Technology & Sciences

Shri Govindram Seksaria Institute of Technology and Science, Indore was established in 1952.SGSITS is recognized as a leading institute in the state of Madhya Pradesh and the western region consistently during its glorious journey of seventy years. It has been ranked in National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) on national level (including IITs and NITs) by MHRD. Further looking to its credentials, All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and University Grant Commission (UGC), New Delhi, has declared the institute as autonomous in 1989. Under the autonomous state, the institute is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi Prodyogiki Vishwavidhyalaya (university of technology of M.P.) Bhopal.

Club Naivedya

Naivedya is an automotive engineering club that competes in SAE-TIFAN to design a Multi-vegetable Transplanter 🚜. The journey so far has been incredible, with vast opportunities for innovation. The team has aced the qualification round into the finals🚀and is all geared up for expansion. We are looking for passionate individuals with a zeal for innovation and a knack for knowledge ✨.

About Multi-Seed transplanter

A multi-seed transplanter is a specialized agricultural machine designed to automate and enhance the process of planting multiple seeds simultaneously in a field. This equipment is commonly used in large-scale farming operations, especially for crops that are traditionally transplanted rather than directly sown as seeds.
Club Naivedya focuses on building a multi-seed transplanter capable of transplanting three different variety of saplings viz. chilli, tomato, brinjal, in any of required manner. Through the design of our transplanter we try to acheieve precise, and firm planting of saplings resilting in higer efficiency at a bare minimum cost.
